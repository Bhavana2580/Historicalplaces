Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World
Heritage Site, is a stunning mausoleum
built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in
memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal,
and is a symbol of love and a
masterpiece of Indo-Islamic architecture

Qutub Minar

The Qutb Minar, a UNESCO World
Heritage Site in Delhi, is a 73-meter (240
feet) tall, five-story minaret and victory
tower

Red Fort

A historic Mughal fort built by Emperor
Shah Jahan in the mid-17th century,
serving as the main residence of the
Mughal Emperors.

Charminar

The Charminar is a monument and mosque
in Hyderabad, India.It was built by
Muhammad Quli Qutb Shahi to celebrate
the end of a deadly plague.
